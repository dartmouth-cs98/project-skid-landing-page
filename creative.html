Frequently
Asked
Questions

Do I need to be good at drawing?

Not at all. Your sketch can be as simple as a quick scribble or motion. What matters is that it feels natural to you and that you're able to replicate it.

What if my drawing changes slightly?

That’s expected. skiD learns your natural variation and recognizes your sketch even if it’s not perfectly identical each time.

Is this still secure?

Yes. Your sketch isn’t just pixels on a screen. Our algorithm captures subtle timing and motion patterns, making it extremely difficult for others to impersonate your sketch.

Where can I use this?

On any modern device: a phone, laptop, or tablet. If you can draw, you can use skiD.
