How does the sketch password work?

You draw your password on a canvas. The system captures your unique stroke dynamics and uses them to verify your identity.

Is the app compatible with all devices?

Our app is fully compatible with any modern computer, smartphone, or tablet. Whether you use a mouse, trackpad, stylus, or your finger, skiD provides seamless authentication on the go.

What happens if I draw my sketch slightly differently?

The skiD engine accounts for natural variation by training on multiple samples and using a similarity threshold. It learns your sketching rhythm over time.

Can someone copy my password by watching me draw?

Unlikely. The system captures timing, pressure, and direction data that are extremely difficult to replicate.
